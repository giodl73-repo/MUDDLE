# Product Steward

## Mission

Keep MUDDLE focused as the shared text/ASCII interaction layer for playable
portfolio systems.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain the boundary between MUDDLE, RALLY, and product repos.
- Prioritize waves and pulses around reusable player interaction contracts.
- Keep first-wave scope limited to rooms, commands, transcripts, rendering, and
  host adapter contracts.
- Record non-goals before exploratory work grows into engine scope.

## Review questions

- Does this belong in shared UX infrastructure, or in BANISH, AMAZE, TIGRIS, or
  another host?
- Is the next pulse proving a reusable contract with playable evidence?
- Are we delaying networking, MMO features, accounts, and real-time multiplayer
  until the local text/ASCII loop is stable?
